Open faced Mediterranean Breakfast Bagel

The Mediterranean Breakfast Bagel is something that I freestyled in the kitchen when I was pinching pennies and was tired of eating the same thing over and over. Now to this day it is something I look forward to making often and remains a staple breakfast item in my household. Here are the key ingredients to making this happen.

Key Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Hummus
  • One Cumbers
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olives
  • Sriracha(optional)
  • Dried Dillweed

You will need a frying pan, toaster, and cutting board for this meal.

Process:

  1. Slice cucumber, cut slices in half
  2. Slice one olive
  3. Crack two eggs in a bowl, add dillweed, splash of milk Beat eggs
  4. Toast bagel
  5. Heat frying pan with butter, add beaten eggs. Scramble, but form eggs into a circle same size as the bagel.
  6. Spread hummus on bagel, add cucumber and olive slices.
  7. Add fetta, sriracha and top it off with your scrambled egg

Bon Appetit!
