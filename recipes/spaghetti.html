Spaghetti

Spaghetti is one of the most simple, yet delicious and satisfying foods that you can make. In order to make a beautiful pot of spaghetti there are a few key Ingredients.

Key Ingredients:

  • Two garlic cloves
  • Yellow Onions
  • Uncooked spaghetti noodles
  • Your choice of Pasta sauce
  • Basil, Rosemary, Italian seasoning, Red pepper flakes
  • Olive Oil

In order to cook these ingredients you will need a large pot for boiling the water, a sauce pan for your spaghetti sauce,and a strainer to strain the water from your noodles. Next is the order in which you will make these items.

Process:

  1. Fill large pot with water
  2. Bring water to a boil
  3. while water is heating up, dice your onions and garlic, add uncoocked pasta noodles once water is finished boiling
  4. heat olive oil in sauce pan
  5. Add garlice, stir until you can smell it
  6. Add onions, stir until translucent
  7. Add pasta sauce and spices
  8. once pasta is cooked properly, strain water from pasta, put pasta back in the pot
  9. mix your pasta sauce and noodle together, portion accordingly

Bon Appetit!
