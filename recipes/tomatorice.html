Tomato Rice

Tomato Rice is another meal I discovered while trying to save money. It is simple, delicious, and packs a sufficient amount of nutrients. You can add or remove ingredients according to your personal taste. Here are some key ingredients:

Key Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Rice or Quinoa
  • Hummus
  • cucumbers
  • Can of Stewed Tomatos
  • Sriracha
  • Feta Cheese

    • You will need and frying pan, and a rice cooker or small pot for rice/ quinoa.

Process:

  1. Put rice/ quinoa in the rice cooker along with stewed tomatos, mix occasionally while cooking
  2. While rice is cooking, cut up your cucumbers
  3. Add 2 eggs, dill and milk in a bowl, mix to scramble
  4. Once rice starts to boil, heat a pan with butter, add egg mixture
  5. Scramble eggs lightly
  6. Add tomamto rice first, then on top section your cucumbers, egg and hummus. Top with feta and sriracha

Bon Appetit!
